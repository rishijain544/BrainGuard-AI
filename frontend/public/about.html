Architecture & Methodology

Discover the sophisticated AI models powering BrainGuard AI's clinical-grade predictions.

1. Convolutional Neural Network (CNN)

Our custom baseline CNN extracts low-level spatial hierarchies from MRI scans. It acts as a fast, robust primary indicator for tumor presence, specifically optimized for edge detection in neurological imaging.

2. ResNet50 (Transfer Learning)

By leveraging a pre-trained ResNet50 architecture, our model benefits from deep residual learning. It effectively handles vanishing gradients, allowing it to capture complex, high-level structural anomalies with extreme precision.

3. Vision Transformer (ViT) Hybrid

The state-of-the-art Hybrid ResNet-ViT architecture segments the image into patches and utilizes self-attention mechanisms. It understands global context and long-range dependencies within the brain scan, a task where traditional CNNs struggle.

Ensemble Inference

We combine the probabilistic outputs of all three architectures to form a highly confident consensus.

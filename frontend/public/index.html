About Brain Guard AI

Discover the cutting-edge technology behind our 3D medical AI diagnostics.

Our Mission

Brain Guard AI is designed to empower medical professionals with lightning-fast, highly accurate tumor detection capabilities. By utilizing a state-of-the-art ensemble of convolutional neural networks and vision transformers, we drastically reduce the time it takes to analyze MRI scans.

Our platform ensures that no detail goes unnoticed, providing robust confidence scores and high-tech visualizations to support clinical decision-making.

How It Works

1

Upload Scan

Upload standard T1, T2, or FLAIR MRI images directly into our secure 3D interface.

2

AI Processing

The image is processed through our CNN, ResNet50, and Hybrid ViT ensemble models simultaneously.

3

3D Analytics

Receive a unified diagnosis, confidence scores, and tumor detection metrics instantly on your dashboard.
